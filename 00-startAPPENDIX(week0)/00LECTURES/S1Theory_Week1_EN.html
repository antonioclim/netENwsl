üìö What Is a Network ?

Definitie

O network of computers is o colectie of dispozitive of calcul interconectate which pot schimba data and partaja resurse. Conexiunea can fi fizic (cabluri, fibre) or wireless (radio, infrarosu).

üìê Topologii Fizice and Logice

Topologie Stea

Switch central; each gazd are conexiune dedicata. Cea mhours comuna in LAN-uri moderne.

Topologie Magistrala

Cablu unic partajat (coaxial). Istoric: Ethernet 10BASE2. Predispus at coliziuni.

Topologie Inel

Token circula; transmisie secventiala. Istoric: Token Ring (IEEE 802.5).

Topologie Logica can diferi of cea fizica: Wi-Fi apare ca stea logic, but e broadcast fizic.

üìö Stiva of Protocoof TCP/IP

‚ö†Ô∏è TCP/IP vs. OSI

TCP/IP Model are 4 layers; model OSI (ISO) are 7 layers. Vom detalia OSI in Week 2. In practice, TCP/IP domin Internetul.

üîç Rolurile Fiecarui Layer

üî¥ Aplicatie

Interfata the userui with the network. Formateaz data for applications specifice (web, email, transfer files).

üü† Transport

Livrare end-to-end. TCP: fiabila, ordonata. UDP: fast, far conexiune. Porturile identific processesle.

üü¢ Network

Rutare between networks. Adresare IP, determinarea pathsi, fragmentation. Livrare ‚Äûbest effort".

üîµ Linka+Fizic

Transfer on un singur link. Adresare MAC, detectare errors (FCS), codare bits on mediu.

üî¥ Intarziere of Procesare (dproc)

üü† Intarziere of Coad (dcoada)

üî¨ Intarziere of Transmisie vs. Intarziere of Propagare

üü¢ Intarziere of Transmisie (dtrans)

Timpul for a impinge toti bitsi packageului on link.

dtrans = L / R

L = dimensiune package (bits), R = width of bandwidth (bits/sec)

üîµ Intarziere of Propagare (dprop)

Timpul for ca un bit to calathoursasca de-a lungul linkului.

dprop = d / s

d = away (m), s = ~2√ó10‚Å∏ m/s in fibra

üí° Observatie Critica

A link of 1 Gbps transmite of 10√ó mhours repede decat 100 Mbps, but delaya of propagare ramane neschimbat ‚Äî limitat of speed luminii. Latenta intercontinental not can scadea sub ~50ms.
